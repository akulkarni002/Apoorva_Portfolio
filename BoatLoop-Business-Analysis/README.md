# BoatLoop Business Analysis Case Study

## Project Overview

BoatLoop is a proposed boat-sharing platform designed to connect private boat owners with passengers seeking recreational and travel experiences on the water.

The project involved performing business analysis activities including stakeholder identification, feasibility assessment, requirements gathering, risk analysis, requirements engineering, and solution design. The objective was to create a secure, scalable, and user-friendly platform that supports boat rentals while maintaining compliance with maritime regulations.

---

## Business Problem

Private boat owners often have underutilized vessels, while passengers face challenges finding accessible and reliable boating experiences.

The objective was to design a digital platform that:

- Connects boat owners and passengers
- Simplifies booking and payment processes
- Supports customer reviews and ratings
- Ensures regulatory compliance
- Protects user data and privacy
- Provides a scalable and sustainable business model

---

## Project Goals

### Business Goals

- Launch the BoatLoop platform within a budget of CAD $15 million
- Support up to 10,000 concurrent users
- Maintain 98% system uptime
- Provide secure and transparent booking experiences
- Create additional revenue opportunities for boat owners

### Success Metrics

- User adoption and engagement
- Booking volume and platform utilization
- Regulatory compliance
- System performance and reliability
- Customer satisfaction

## Process Analysis

The platform was analyzed across several core business processes.

### Account Management

Users create and verify accounts before accessing platform services.

### Boat Listing Management

Boat owners publish listings containing vessel specifications, trip details, and services.

### Booking Management

Passengers search, select, and reserve available boats.

### Payment Processing

The platform securely processes transactions and confirms bookings.

### Trip Completion

Completed trips trigger reviews, ratings, and financial settlements.

### Customer Support

Support agents assist with bookings, cancellations, disputes, and service requests.
