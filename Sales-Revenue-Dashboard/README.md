# Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard
Dashboard Preview




Project Overview

Developed an interactive Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard to evaluate business performance across product categories from 2003–2005.

The project transforms raw sales transaction data into actionable business insights through KPI reporting, trend analysis, variance reporting, and data visualization.

Business Problem

Management required visibility into:

Revenue performance over time
Product category profitability
Revenue contribution by deal size
Seasonal sales trends
Overall business performance

The objective was to support data-driven decision-making using historical sales data.

Analysis Performed
Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis

Identified seasonal revenue patterns and peak sales periods.

Product Category Analysis

Compared revenue performance across six product categories.

Deal Size Analysis

Evaluated revenue contributions from Small, Medium, and Large deals.

Variance Reporting

Developed supporting variance reports to monitor performance fluctuations.

Key Findings
Classic Cars generated over $3.9M in revenue
Revenue peaked in November 2003 and November 2004
Medium-sized deals produced the highest revenue contribution
Maritime & Rail generated the weakest large-deal revenue performance
Business Recommendations
Increase investment in high-performing Classic Cars products
Investigate drivers behind strong November sales performance
Expand medium-sized sales opportunities
Review pricing and sales strategy for Maritime & Rail products
